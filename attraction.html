SPECIAL SSB INTERVIEW COACHING BATCH FOR SAINIK SCHOOLIANS

During summer vacation students of Std XI & XII of Sainik schools can come to AISSOSA Patna center to take SSB interview coaching for one month preferably from 20 May to 20 June every year. It is called summer vacation special SSB interview coaching for Sainik schoolians. In this course Sainik schoolians are provided free coaching & free hostel.
For attending summer vacation special SSB interview coaching the students of Std XI & XII of Sainik schools have to bring mattress because here only bed is provided.
During summer vacation special SSB interview coaching mess/ tiffin facility/ washer man/ medical treatment is available on payment to interested students.
Interested Sainik schoolians are to get their free coaching seat booked latest by 30 April of that year.
The present students & EX-Students of Sainik schools if they come for other batches of SSB. Then they have to pay 50% of the fee which comes to Rs 3000/-.
